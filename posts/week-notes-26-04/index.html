Here’s everything I did last week

Fediverse stuff

I stumbled upon omg.lol and found it really interesting. After a few minutes of checking out the services that were offered, I joined and created a profile page, a now page and a weblog. I also joined social.lol and it has some of the nicest people there. I’m really digging this whole ecosystem.

Domain registrar switch

Realized I was paying a little too much for my domain at Namecheap. And they kept increasing their prices. Found out about porkbun and saw some good reviews by the fine folks at social.lol and ended up switching. The whole migration took about 15 minutes and it was really smooth.
